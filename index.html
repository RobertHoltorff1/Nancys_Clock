⚙ Settings

Her name
Reminder interval (minutes)

How many minutes between bathroom reminders. Default: 150 (= 2½ hours). The timer resets each time she presses "I'm headed to the bathroom now" or dismisses an alarm.

Default clip length (seconds)

Used for any message that doesn't specify its own clip length. The chime plays once, full length, at full volume — then loops quietly at that same length until the next full-volume play, 2 min 30 sec after the first one started.

Quiet-loop volume (%)

How loud the looping background chime is between full-volume plays, as a percent of full volume. 50% ≈ 6 dB quieter (roughly half as loud).

Morning pills message
